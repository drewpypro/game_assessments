# Assessment: moonlighters 2

**Type:** Game  
**Date:** 2025-12-01T00:00:00Z  
**Overall Score:** 4/5  

---

## Key Summary

Moonlighter 2: The Endless Vault is a sequel to the well‑regarded shopkeeping roguelike, developed by Spanish studio Digital Sun and published by experienced indie publisher 11 bit studios, giving it a strong and reputable identity. There is no indication of invasive anti‑cheat or heavy third‑party DRM beyond standard Steam protections, which should be acceptable to most PC players. Early Steam reviews are Very Positive at around 83%, with players praising the core loop and upgraded presentation while acknowledging some balance and pacing issues typical of an evolving roguelike. Overall, the game appears to be a solid, recommended choice for fans of action‑RPG roguelites and shopkeeping sims, with room for refinement as updates continue.

Steam label: **Very Positive**  
Positive review %: **83%**
