# Assessment: phantom brigade

**Date:** 2025-11-29T00:00:00Z

## Key Summary

Phantom Brigade is a tactical mech strategy game developed and published by Canadian indie studio Brace Yourself Games, an established and reputable developer with a history of successful titles. The game appears to rely only on standard platform DRM and does not use third‑party or kernel‑level anti‑cheat, making it effectively non‑intrusive from an anti‑cheat/DRM standpoint. Community sentiment on Steam is generally favorable, with a "Mostly Positive" overall rating and improving recent reviews that highlight its unique timeline‑based combat and customization, while noting some concerns around campaign structure and variety that the recent 2.0 overhaul seeks to improve.

### Anti-Cheat & Client Risk (Score: 90/5)

Available public information indicates that Phantom Brigade is a single‑player tactical RPG without any bundled third‑party anti‑cheat or intrusive DRM such as Denuvo or kernel‑level services. The Steam and Epic Games Store pages list standard platform features (achievements, cloud saves, etc.) but do not mention additional DRM or anti‑cheat components, and there is no indication from common reference sites or patch notes that such systems are used. As a result, the game is best classified as having no notable anti‑cheat and only standard store‑level DRM.

### Community Sentiment (Score: 80/5)

Steam label: **Mostly Positive**  
Positive review %: **77%**

On Steam, Phantom Brigade currently holds an overall "Mostly Positive" user review label, with approximately 77% of all user reviews rated positive and a recent 30‑day slice showing "Very Positive" sentiment at around 83%. Players frequently praise the innovative timeline‑based mech combat, strong visuals, and deep customization, while common criticisms focus on earlier campaign structure, mission variety, and AI behavior—issues that recent major updates (including the 2.0 overhaul) aim to address. Overall, community sentiment is solidly positive, especially among fans of tactical mech strategy games.
