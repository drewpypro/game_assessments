# Assessment: windrose

**Type:** Game  
**Date:** 2026-02-22T00:00:00Z  
**Overall Score:** 4/5  

---

## Key Summary

Windrose is an upcoming pirate-themed survival action-adventure game developed and self-published by Windrose Crew, a studio based in Tashkent, Uzbekistan, with a growing but still relatively young reputation. Identity and ownership are clear and well-documented, and the project has been showcased at notable events and covered by mainstream gaming press, supporting a strong confidence score in this dimension. Community sentiment around the demo and playtest is very positive, with over 90% of Steam reviews recommending the game and players highlighting its naval combat, atmosphere, and survival-crafting systems, though some note that combat tuning and polish are still in progress. The main uncertainty lies in the anti-cheat/DRM dimension: there is no explicit indication of invasive or kernel-level solutions, but also no definitive technical documentation yet, so this area remains unknown rather than clearly safe or problematic. Overall, Windrose currently appears promising and well-received, meriting a positive but slightly cautious overall score pending more concrete information on its final anti-cheat/DRM implementation and full release state.

## Dimensions

### Identity & Ownership (Score: 5/5)

Windrose is a pirate-themed survival action-adventure game developed and self-published by Windrose Crew, a studio headquartered in Tashkent, Uzbekistan. The Steam store page lists Windrose Crew as both developer and publisher, and external coverage describes the team (formerly Crosswind Crew) as an emerging but increasingly visible studio in the survival-crafting space. While not yet an established AAA name, the project has been featured in events like the PC Gaming Show and Steam Next Fest, suggesting a growing profile and generally professional presentation aligned with expectations for a mid-sized indie/AA online survival title.

**Publisher:** Windrose Crew  
**Country:** Uzbekistan

### Anti-Cheat & Client Risk (Score: 2/5)

Public information about Windrose’s anti-cheat or DRM implementation is currently limited. The Steam store and SteamDB entries do not list any third-party DRM such as Denuvo, nor do they mention kernel-level or always-on anti-cheat solutions, but this absence is not definitive given the game is still unreleased and in demo/playtest stages. Until the developers publish explicit details or PCGamingWiki and official FAQs document the launch build, the exact anti-cheat/DRM setup and its intrusiveness remain unknown.

**Anti-cheat type:** unknown  
**Classification:** unknown

### Community Sentiment (Score: 5/5)

Steam label: **Very Positive**  
Positive review %: **93-94%**

As of late February 2026, Windrose does not yet have a released full version on Steam, but its demo and playtest have attracted strong early reception. Reporting based on Steam data notes roughly 94% positive reviews from over 400 demo reviews shortly after launch, and SteamDB lists an overall review percentage around 93% for the playtest, corresponding to a "Very Positive" style sentiment label. Early community feedback frequently praises the pirate atmosphere, ship combat, and survival-crafting loop, while some players point out that combat balance and technical polish still need refinement before full release.
