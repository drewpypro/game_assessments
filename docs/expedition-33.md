# Assessment: expedition 33

**Type:** Game  
**Date:** 2025-12-19T00:00:00Z  
**Overall Score:** 5/5  

---

## Key Summary

Clair Obscur: Expedition 33 is a highly acclaimed single‑player RPG developed by French studio Sandfall Interactive and published by Kepler Interactive, with strong evidence of reputable, well‑documented ownership. The PC release appears to rely only on standard platform DRM without any dedicated or kernel‑level anti‑cheat, suggesting a low‑intrusion setup for players. Community sentiment on Steam is overwhelmingly positive, with roughly 95% recent positive reviews and broad praise for its art, storytelling, and combat, making it a strongly recommended title overall.

---

## Dimensions

### Identity & Ownership (Score: 5/5)

Clair Obscur: Expedition 33 is the debut title from Sandfall Interactive, a French studio based in Montpellier, and is published globally on PC and consoles by Kepler Interactive. Both the Steam and Epic Games Store pages list Sandfall Interactive as developer and Kepler Interactive as publisher, while coverage in major outlets and the official site highlight the game as a French production that has become a national point of pride. Overall, the developer and publisher appear reputable and well‑established, with strong critical reception and significant commercial success reinforcing their credibility.

**Developer:** Sandfall Interactive  
**Publisher:** Kepler Interactive  
**Country:** France

---

### Anti-Cheat & Client Risk (Score: 4/5)

On PC, Clair Obscur: Expedition 33 is a purely single‑player turn‑based RPG and does not advertise any third‑party anti‑cheat middleware such as Easy Anti‑Cheat, BattlEye, or kernel‑level drivers, nor does the Steam store page indicate additional DRM beyond standard Steam integration. No reputable sources or community documentation report the presence of Denuvo or other invasive DRM, and technical coverage focuses on performance, Deck verification, and patches rather than anti‑cheat. Based on available information, the game appears to use only standard platform DRM and no dedicated anti‑cheat, implying a non‑intrusive setup for players.

**Anti-cheat type:** none  
**Classification:** standard

---

### Community Sentiment (Score: 5/5)

Steam label: **Overwhelmingly Positive**  
Positive review %: **95%**

On Steam, Clair Obscur: Expedition 33 holds an "Overwhelmingly Positive" user review summary, with recent reviews showing about 95% positive and overall reviews similarly high across more than 100,000 total ratings. Players consistently praise the game’s art direction, emotionally resonant story, soundtrack, and inventive blend of turn‑based and real‑time combat, while common criticisms focus on performance issues at launch and some balance quirks that have been addressed through patches. Overall community sentiment is very strong, with the game widely regarded as one of the standout RPGs of 2025.
